# Sample Code of Conduct for Testing

## Labor Standards

### Child Labor
We prohibit the use of child labor in any form. Suppliers must verify the age of all employees.

### Working Conditions
- Safe working environment required
- Maximum 48-hour work week
- Overtime must be voluntary and compensated
- Workers have right to form unions

## Environmental Standards

### Pollution Control
Suppliers must:
- Obtain all required environmental permits
- Monitor and report emissions
- Implement waste reduction programs
- Prevent water pollution

### Violations
Any fines or violations of environmental regulations must be reported within 30 days.

## Governance & Ethics

### Anti-Corruption
Zero tolerance for bribery, corruption, or conflicts of interest.

### Transparency
Suppliers must maintain accurate records and allow audits.
